Welcome to NYU B&F

NYU B&F is a unique student organization devoted to expanding students' understanding of blockchain technology and the FinTech ecosystem, as well as the legal, economic, social, and political challenges posed by these innovative technologies in the US and globally.

The organization also connects and prepares NYU students for careers in the FinTech and blockchain industry. We have a vibrant community of students, professionals, and faculty at NYU.
